How to play

  1. Click Start Game to begin the 3-minute challenge.
  2. Click a word to send it to the next space, or drag it if you prefer.
  3. Click Check Answer to see if the order is correct.
  4. You can only move to the next sentence after a correct answer.
  5. Try to finish all 7 sentences before the timer reaches zero.
