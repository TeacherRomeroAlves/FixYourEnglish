Verb Tense Lesson

THE FAMOUS VERB TO BE

We use the verb to be in the simple present to talk about names, age, feelings, jobs, nationality, location, and descriptions.

😊 Feelings 📍 Location 🎂 Age 💼 Jobs

When To Use It

Simple but very important

Name
Age
Place
Feeling
  • Identity: I am Lucas.
  • Age: She is 14 years old.
  • Location: They are at school.
  • Feeling: We are tired today.
  • Negative: He is not at home.
  • Question: Are you ready?

Verb To Be Chart

Affirmative, Negative, and Questions

Form
Structure
Examples
Affirmative
I + am
He / She / It + is
You / We / They + are
I am a student.
This cellphone is expensive.
They are in class.
Negative
I + am not
He / She / It + is not
You / We / They + are not
I am not tired.
My car isn't red.
We aren't late.
Questions
Am + I ...?
Is + he / she / it ...?
Are + you / we / they ...?
Wh word + am / is / are + subject ...?
Are you ready? Yes, I am.
Is she at school? No, she isn't.
Where are they? They are at home.
How old is he? He is 13.

Reading Activity

Read and Choose True or False

Read the text about Sofia's family. Then choose True or False for each sentence.

Sofia is 14 years old and she is from Brazil. She is a student at a small school in her town. Her mother is a nurse and her father is a bus driver. They are very busy during the week, but they are happy together. Sofia's older brother is 20 years old and he is a college student. He is not at home in the mornings because his classes are early. Sofia's little sister is only 6 years old and she is very funny. The children are close, and they are good friends too. Their grandparents are not in the same city, but they are always in contact by phone.

1. Sofia is 14 years old.

2. Sofia is from Canada.

3. Her mother is a nurse.

4. Her father is not a bus driver.

5. Her older brother is a college student.

6. Her little sister is not funny.

7. Their grandparents are not in the same city.

Choose True or False for all seven sentences and then check your answers.
