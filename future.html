Future Chart

Affirmative, Negative, and Questions

Form
Structure
Examples
Affirmative
Subject + will + base verb
Subject + am / is / are + going to + base verb
I will call you later.
She is going to travel next month.
They are going to study tonight.
Negative
Subject + will not + base verb
Subject + am / is / are + not + going to + base verb
I will not forget your birthday.
He is not going to play today.
Questions
Will + subject + base verb?
Am / Is / Are + subject + going to + base verb?
Wh word + will / am / is / are + subject + base verb?
Will they come tomorrow? Yes, they will.
Is she going to study tonight? No, she isn't.
What will you do later? I will do my homework.
Where are they going to travel? They are going to travel to Bahia.
